Triathlete

In 2014, I had a very scary experience swimming in the Stockbridge Bowl in Lenox, MA. Afterwards, I resolved to become a better swimmer, and in March of 2015, as a birthday present from my wonderful girlfriend, I began to take swimming lessons, and at the same time signed up for my first triathlon (go big or go home). In September of 2015, I raced the Buzzards Bay Sprint Triathlon, and after crossing the finish line, I was hooked. Though swimming is still not my favorite way to spend an afternoon, I get deep satisfaction from both the process of training and the act of racing.
